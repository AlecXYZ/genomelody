Synthesizer

Transform DNA information from a FASTA file into sound using a chosen algorithm. After the conversion, it will be represented as MIDI and WAV audio files.

Piano Player

Play transformed DNA information as sound and visualize each note on a piano. All that is required are MIDI and WAV audio files generated from the synthesizer.
