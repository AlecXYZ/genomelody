Discover the Sound of DNA

Tutorial

Follow a guided tutorial that walks you through the process of transforming DNA sequences into music. This mode combines the Synthesizer and Player tools.

Synthesizer

Convert DNA sequence from FASTA file into music by selecting from multiple mapping algorithms, tempo settings, and instrument sounds. The tool generates both MIDI and WAV files.

Comparator

Compare two DNA sequences to find their differences, then generate audio where corresponding notes from each sequence are played simultaneously to highlight the contrast.

Player

Load and play your generated MIDI and WAV files in the Player, which includes a piano visualization. This tool allows you to experience DNA music both audibly and visually.
